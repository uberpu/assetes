# ARCANA GRID TACTICS — LANDSCAPE GENERATOR PROMPT

You are generating a **landscape map** for Arcana Grid Tactics.

## Purpose

The landscape exists to **force interaction**, create pressure around towers, and reward positional play. It is not decorative.

## Grid

- Square grid topology
- May be irregular, with missing tiles
- Elevation and terrain are mandatory

## Required Outputs

- Tile coordinates
- Terrain type per tile
- Elevation per tile
- Tower locations
- Hazards and choke points
- Regions for future systems

## Constraints

- All players must have paths to conflict
- Towers must be defensible but contestable
- No permanent isolation
- Terrain must shape movement and stance cycles

## Design Guidance

- Narrow paths increase conflict
- High ground attracts pressure
- Hazards reward forced movement

## Output Rules

- Structured data only
- No art, lighting, or camera instructions
- Deterministic and engine-ready

Your goal is to create a **playable battlefield**, not a scenic map.
